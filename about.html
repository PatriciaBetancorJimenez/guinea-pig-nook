Story

The idea of stablishing a guinea pig shop began back in the 70's when we the founders were still university students and found out that we could merge our knowledge to create something that the three of us have passion about: animals, independence and creativity. After no long, one of our colleagues started searching for a suitible location in Cheltenham. The bussiness has been opened since 1972.

Shop

Our shop is located in the very center of a beautiful town named Cheltenham within easy reach of the main cities in the south west midlands. Though its central position, the store is yet based on an idillyc and bucolic place far away from the noise of the town. The wild winters and summers enable the guinea pigs to have a perfect quality of life. Do you life far away? Don't worry, you can purchase your guinea pig online.

Environment

As animal lovers, we are extremely aware of the environmetal impact the human being causes to Earth. For this reason, we aim to reduce the shop's wastes to the minimum by recycling and have even recently implaced solar panels to reduce the power consumption as well as a sewage water depuration system. Though the invest costs for this instalations were high, we are now proud to say that we are a green energy bussiness.
