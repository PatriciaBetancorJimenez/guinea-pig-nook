General Conditions

Online purchase Return conditions Stock availability Contact
We do offer the possibility of purchasing your pet online. One of our staff members will come over to the specified delivery address to bring your guinea pig. Please, be aware that we will charge you an additional fee of 10£ for this service if you live in Gloucestershire. If you live somewhere else in the UK, you will be charged for 20£.
You have 1 week after purchase to give your guinea pig back either for a refund or exchange. We would just need you to bring your proof of purchase. We also offer a guarantee period for 6 months in the case that the guinea pig has any health poblem that our vet is not able to heal. Bear in mind that you will need to bring your pet to the shop for the animal to be checked. A proof of purchase will be needed for this as well.
We are unable to know when a certain breed, color or sex will be available for purchase as our suppliers usually vary. We suggest that you contact us if you are looking for any specific guinea pig and we will try our best to satisfy your demand. However, the stock that we show on our website is the stock that we currently have.
